Our journey began in 2012 when my brother and I started volunteering at a local primary school teaching math and English. We realized that there was a skills gap in our education system where kids finish school with no applicable skills at all. We started the TODIEA LTD. TODIDEA stands for teachers on devices. We are currently conducting coding lessons in over 20 primary schools in the Kampala area under our partnership with the city council.

Our vision is to expose every child in Uganda with a computer science education by the time they are 12 years old.
